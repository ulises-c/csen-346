* Clearly explains the proposed method or implementation.  
* Demonstrates strong understanding of underlying concepts and algorithms.

**3\. Experimental Results & Analysis (20%)**

* Proper labeling of graphs, tables, and visuals.

**6\. Delivery & Communication (10%)**

* Clear, confident, and professional delivery.  
* Good pacing and time management.

**7\. Q\&A & Conceptual Understanding (10%)**
